Evangelion is set in a post-apocalyptic Tokyo-3, 15 years after a global cataclysm known as the "Second Impact." The story centers on Shinji Ikari, a teenage boy recruited by his estranged and cold father, Gendo Ikari, to join the special paramilitary organization NERV. NERV's purpose is to defend humanity from mysterious, monstrous beings known as "Angels" using giant bio-machines called "Evangelions." Shinji is forced to become the pilot of Evangelion Unit-01, a task he resents but is psychologically bonded to.

Plot

The series follows Shinji and the other teenage pilots, the stoic Rei Ayanami and the fiery Asuka Langley Soryu, as they battle the increasingly bizarre and powerful Angels. However, the battles serve as a backdrop for a deep, psychological drama exploring the pilots' trauma, depression, and difficulty in connecting with other people. As the story progresses, Shinji and the others uncover the dark conspiracies of NERV and the shadowy organization SEELE, all revolving around the "Human Instrumentality Project," an enigmatic plan to force the next stage of human evolution.
